Description

The ability to effectively and efficiently use time is essential skill for every manager and team leader. Time management is the act or process of planning and exercising conscious control over the amount of time spent on specific activities, especially to increase effectiveness, efficiency or productivity. Time management may be aided by a range of skills, tools, and techniques used to manage time when accomplishing specific tasks, projects and goals complying with a due date. Often it may seem that there isn't enough time to do everything that you need to. This can lead to a build-up of stress. It's important for managers of people to develop effective strategies for managing time in order to balance the conflicting demands of professional and personal life and focus on tasks and activities that are important. This course looks at the effective use of time, how to prioritise work and allocate the right amount to activities.
